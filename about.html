We believes outdoors is for everyone.

Our Story

Camping used to feel complicated. Heavy packs, complex poles, and uncomfortable nights. We wanted to change that. Vista Camp was born out of a desire to slow down. We design gear for the weekenders, the sun-seekers, and those who believe a night under the stars should be as comfortable as a night in a hotel.

Our Philosophy

We strip away the complexity of "survival" gear and focus on what matters: connection. Connection with nature, with friends, and with yourself. Our products are simple to use, beautiful to look at, and built to create memories.

Premium gear for the casual camper. Making the outdoors accessible and beautiful.
